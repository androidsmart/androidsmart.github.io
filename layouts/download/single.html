The Automotive Industry The automotive industry is a global and competitive sector that involves the manufacturing, marketing, and selling of automotive vehicles and related parts and services. The automotive industry is one of the largest and most influential industries in the world, as it contributes to the economic growth, employment, innovation, and trade of many countries. According to the International Organization of Motor Vehicle Manufacturers (OICA), the automotive industry produced about 78 million vehicles worldwide in 2020, with China, the United States, Japan, Germany, and India being the top five producers. The automotive industry is also constantly evolving and adapting to the changing needs and preferences of consumers, as well as the environmental and social challenges that affect the world.
