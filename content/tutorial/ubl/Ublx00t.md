## Method 1: Unlock Bootloader without PC

This method is very simple and convenient, as you only need to install an official app from Asus and follow the instructions. However, this method only works for Android 8 and 9 versions. If you have updated your device to Android 10, you will need to use the second method.

Here are the steps to unlock the bootloader without PC:

- Download the Unlock Device App from Asus website and install it on your device.
- Open the app and accept the terms and conditions. Tap on the "Press to unlock your device" button.
- Wait for the app to verify your device and send the unlock request to Asus server.
- Once the unlock request is approved, your device will reboot automatically and enter the fastboot mode.
- You will see a message on the screen saying "Press any key to continue". Press any key on your device to complete the unlock process.
- Your device will reboot again and show a warning message that your device is unlocked. Tap on "OK" to proceed.
- Congratulations, you have successfully unlocked the bootloader on your Asus Zenfone Max Pro M1.
