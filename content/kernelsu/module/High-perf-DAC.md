**High perf DAC** kernelsu module is a Magisk/KernelSU module that enables the High Performance DAC mode of Qualcomm's WCD9xx DAC to maximize its power and get the best audio quality possible.

## How this works

enables the "High Performance DAC" mode of Qualcomm's WCD9xx DAC to maximize its power and get the best audio quality possible. 
 

## Qualcomm's WCD9xx DAC

Qualcomm's WCD9xx DAC is a built-in audio chip that converts digital signals to analog sounds. It is present in every phone powered by a Qualcomm processor, but it is rarely configured to be used to its full potential by OEMs.

## difference between the normal and high performance modes of the DAC

By default, the DAC operates in a normal mode that limits its output voltage and current to save battery and reduce distortion. However, this also reduces the volume and clarity of the audio output. By enabling the high performance mode, the DAC can boost its output voltage and current, and deliver a more immersive and rich listening experience.

## conclusion

Using the high perf DAC magisk/kernelsu module can enhance the audio quality of your Qualcomm-powered device in various ways. You can enjoy louder and clearer sounds, with more details and depth. You can also experience a wider dynamic range, which means the difference between the quietest and loudest sounds will be more noticeable.
