LiteGapps is a popular open-source project that provides customized Google Apps packages for Android devices. Why Choose LiteGapps?

  • Reduced Size: LiteGapps packages are significantly smaller than the standard Google Apps, saving valuable storage space on your device.
  • Improved Performance: Fewer apps and services running in the background translate to improved system performance and battery life.
  • Customization: LiteGapps allows you to choose the specific Google apps you need, giving you more control over your Android experience.
  • Open Source: The project is open source, ensuring transparency and community-driven development.
