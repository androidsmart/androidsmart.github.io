Certainly! Let's delve into the world of kernels for the **Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1**. This device, launched in India on April 23, is a lower mid-range smartphone that competes with the Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro in terms of specifications while offering an attractive price point. Here's a deeper look at the kernels available for this device:

1. **Project Infinity (PI) Kernel**:
   - **Description**: This kernel aims for stability, smooth performance, and efficiency.
   - **Base**: It's based on the **CAF (Code Aurora Forum)** source with **4.4.y** Linux upstream changes merged in (except for CAF variants, which are updated directly from CodeAurora).
   - **Variants**: Available for **all variants** of the Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1 (ASUS_X00T).
   - **Community Rating**: **9.4** out of 10.

2. **Darkonah Kernel**:
   - **Features**: This kernel comes packed with various features for the Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1.
   - **Availability**: Specifically designed for this device.
   - **Community Rating**: **9.3** out of 10.

3. **Tea Kernel**:
   - **Description**: A **pure CAF kernel** for the Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1.
   - **Availability**: Tailored for this device.
   - **Community Rating**: **9.0** out of 10.

4. **Smokey Kernel**:
   - **Features**: Smokey Kernel for X00T/T.
   - **Channel and Support Group**: You can find it on **@smokeyrelease**.
   - **Community Rating**: **8.6** out of 10.

5. **Overdose Kernel**:
   - **Description**: A stability-focused kernel for X00T (ASUS Zenfone Max Pro M1).
   - **Archived Version**: **R38.2**.
   - **Community Rating**: **8.6** out of 10.

6. **Genos Kernel**:
   - **Features**: Latest CAF msm-4.9 kernel fully merged, coretech drivers from OnePlus 6 for Oxygen OS users, and more.
   - **Availability**: Designed for **Xiaomi** devices.
   - **Community Rating**: **8.6** out of 10.

7. **Ruega Kernel X00T (TOM)**:
   - **Telegram Group**: You can find it on **@wzrdgrp**.
   - **Availability**: Specifically for the Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1.
   - **Community Rating**: **8.5** out of 10.

8. **Foxy Kernel Lavender**:
   - **Description**: Foxy Kernel for **Redmi Note 7**.
   - **Community Rating**: **8.5** out of 10.

9. **Potato Kernel**:
   - **Features**: Better gaming performance and battery-saving optimizations.
   - **Availability**: For the Asus ZenFone Max Pro M1 (X00TD).
   - **Community Rating**: **8.3** out of 10.

These kernels not only enhance performance but also contribute to the development community around the ZenFone Max Pro M1.
